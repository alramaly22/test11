About Us

Coach Ramy Roshdy began his fitness journey in 1999. With over two decades of hands-on experience, he has competed in some of the world’s most prestigious bodybuilding events, including Musclemania and Dubai Pro (2022 & 2023).

Though he holds a degree in Business Administration, Ramy’s true passion has always been fitness. His mission is to help others transform their lives — both physically and mentally — through proper training and expert guidance.

Whether you're just getting started or chasing elite performance, Coach Ramy will guide you every step of the way with discipline, motivation, and deep expertise.

Absolutely. My coaching isn't just about workouts — it's about full lifestyle support. Whether you need motivation, time management tips, or even help with daily habits, I’ll be there as a brother and a mentor, not just a trainer.

Definitely. I’ve been in this field for over 20 years, and I’ve competed on international stages like Dubai Pro (2022 & 2023) and Musclemania. This is not just a job for me — it’s a lifetime of dedication and passion that I now bring to your journey.

100%. If you commit, I’ll match that energy. My role is to push you, support you, and guide you — not just inside the gym, but in your whole life. Real change happens when you have the right mindset and the right coach. And I’m here to make sure you have both.
